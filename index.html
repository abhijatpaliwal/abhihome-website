The Craft Behind the Brand

3rd

Family Heritage

Three Generations
of Indian Craft

What began as a family passion for textile craftsmanship has grown, generation by generation, into one of India's most certified sustainable home textiles manufacturers. Three generations of knowledge — passed from looms to ledgers — shape every product we make today.

Today, Abhijat Paliwal leads the business with the same values instilled by his family: uncompromising quality, honest manufacturing, and a genuine belief that beautiful things should be made responsibly.
