Design

A throw is the most versatile piece in any room — draped on a chair, layered at the end of a bed, or folded on a sofa, it changes the feel of a space instantly. Our collection spans cotton gauze and linen for a light, breezy drape, through to richer boucle weaves and block-printed designs for depth and artisan character. All woven in Panipat, OEKO-TEX certified, and available for wholesale and private label.
