Our Sustainability Philosophy

Crafted
Responsibly

At Abhi Home®, sustainability is not an afterthought—it's woven into every process. From organic raw material sourcing to certified ethical manufacturing and transparent supply chain management, we hold ourselves accountable to the world's most rigorous standards. Every certificate represents independent verification and ongoing commitment to people and planet.

Our Certifications

Independently Verified
Against Global Standards

Sedex
Supply Chain Ethics & Transparency
Sedex is the world's leading ethics and compliance data management platform. Our membership demonstrates commitment to responsible supply chain practices including labour standards, health & safety, and environmental management.
ISO 9001:2015
Quality Management System
This international standard certifies our quality management system. We maintain consistent product quality, customer satisfaction, and continuous process improvement across all manufacturing operations.
ISO 14001
Environmental Management System
ISO 14001 certification proves our commitment to environmental responsibility. We systematically manage waste, energy use, and environmental impact across all manufacturing facilities.
OEKO-TEX Standard 100
Textile Safety & Sustainability
OEKO-TEX certifies that our textiles are free from harmful substances and safe for human use. Every product is tested against 200+ chemical parameters to ensure the highest safety standards.
GRS (Global Recycled Standard)
Recycled Material Integrity
GRS certification ensures our recycled fibres are traced from collection through processing. This tracking guarantees the integrity and sustainability of every recycled material in our products.
GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)
Organic Fiber Certification
GOTS is the leading certification for organic fibres. It certifies that our organic cotton and other organic materials meet strict environmental and social criteria from farm to finished product.
OCS 100 (Organic Content Standard)
Organic Content Verification
OCS 100 independently verifies that our products contain certified organic material. This standard ensures transparency and prevents greenwashing in organic textile claims.
BCI (Better Cotton Initiative)
Responsible Cotton Sourcing
BCI membership demonstrates our commitment to sourcing cotton that is grown using more sustainable farming practices — reducing water use, limiting harmful chemicals, and improving livelihoods for cotton farmers worldwide. Better Cotton is traceable back to farm level.
Higg Index
Supply Chain Sustainability Score
The Higg Index measures our facility's environmental and social performance. Our verification demonstrates sustainable practices across facility operations, labour practices, and environmental stewardship.
